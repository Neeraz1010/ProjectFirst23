Is this a request for a new or a returning patient?


    


If you are returning patient, please schedule your appointment with the provider that you usually see at our clinics.


A few steps after you schedule a telehealth visit:

1.You will receive a text or email from us confirming your appointment date and time.
2.You will also receive a link through text or email to fill out your registration paperwork prior to your appointment and provide the best way to reach you for your visit.
3.We will contact you 10 minutes before your appointment to work through any missing paperwork and for you to pay for your visit.
4.Your provider will call you to complete your visit.
